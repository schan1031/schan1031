Chile Recipe

  • Description

  • Directions

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds ground beef
  • 2 cloves of garlic, chopped
  • 8 ounce can tomato sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground oregano
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 15 ounce can kidney beans
  • chopped onions
  • chopped green pepper

Description

Chile is a great mix of beans, tomato sauce, meat, onion, peppers and much more. It goes great with rice, or on top of tortilla chips as nachos with cheese and guacamole.

Chili

Cooking Directions

  1. Chop up the onions and peppers
  2. Brown the ground beef by itself
  3. Pour all the ingredients into a large pot and let slow boil
  4. Let is cook for several hours until everything is cooked through
  5. Make sure to let cool before serving
